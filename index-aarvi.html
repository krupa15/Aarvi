About Us

Aarvi, being an app for all those corner grocery shops we visited throughout our childhood and not only bought products but happiness alongwith. Its our time to bring that smile on their face by making them visible online. Giving them the opportunity to be in the technical race.

And whats the issue?

Reminds me of my childhood when I knew a man, very kind, always smiling and gave me a free chocolate every Sunday(main reason to remember).

Very suddenly, times changed and the man like him started to disappear.

I being the one willing to help them to uplift their business and keep it perpetual, has came up the only thing they lack in. Touch of technology.

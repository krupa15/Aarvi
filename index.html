About Us

Aarvi, being an app for all those corner grocery shops we visited throughout our childhood and not only bought products but happiness alongwith. Its our time to bring that smile on their face by making them visible online. Giving them the opportunity to be in the technical race.

A small group of dynamic young people, now a family. A perfect blend of technology and innovation. We not only generate ideas but use technology to make them alive. Here, we are on our way to solve a social issue hence we decided to use our tech skills to serve the society that has been serving us all along. We are on the way to make the society better, ultimately making it a better place for us to dwell in.

And what is that issue? It involves business that are suffocated as they failed to update themselves. So lets serve them with technology as oxygen to revive and be in the race. Business that have legacy ,values, morals, aroma of our culture are now facing problem just because of lack in technology. Business that served us for years are now under threat. So its our time to get them back on track with our tech skills.

Remembering the good old days, when I knew a man, always smiling ,very kind and gave me a free toffee every Sunday when I visited the store with my mom. That corner grocery store, every one has their own stories about it I bet! Visiting the store to buy chocolates from your piggy bank savings was just priceless. Visiting the store to buy chewing gums just to show off the free tattoo was just another level joy !

Very suddenly times changed with a wave of technology and that kind men and corner grocery stores gradually began to disappear. They were highly affected by the technological advancements and due to lack of knowledge and lack of readiness to invest in technology ,they are out of race. Why not bring them back? We want to keep the legacy of business going . We want to keep that joy and smiles intact!

In a nutshell, AARVI is a WEBAPP that will aid all those corner grocery stores to be visible online and will have a chance to be back in the race. The best party about this is it is pocket friendly, as it is for a betterment purpose and just a token thanks to all those corner grocery stores that gave us a lot of memories and aid them to keep the business of their forefathers perpetual.
